EVEN GROUND

2026

Even Ground is an introspective debut that navigates the tension between control and release. Seven tracks of psychedelic rock and jazz fusion, shaped by expressive guitar work and a deep sensitivity to space. The album unfolds slowly, rewarding patience with layers of texture and emotional nuance that reveal themselves on repeated listens.

ROVINA 7

SAUDADE

2026

Saudade — the Portuguese word for a longing that carries beauty in its melancholy. This EP distils that feeling into five atmospheric pieces. Sky and light filtered through half-open blinds: impressions of distance, memory, and the ache of something just out of reach. A quieter, more ambient side of Rovina 7.

  2. Blue Hour
